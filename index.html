Shaded Showdown

Two player local Fighting Game for Interactive Computing class. Created by Steven, Joe, Peter, Sam

Characters

Kitsune

More focused on keeping the distance with ranged attacks. She has the least amount of health and the slowest movement speed of the characters

Basic Attack

A fast tail swipe dealing fast melee damage directly in front of her

Heavy Attack

Quickly fires a fast-moving, small fireball

Special Attack

Floats while charging the attack. After a small delay, fires 3 slow large fireballs

Raven

Focused on speed and agility in movement, with fast melee attacks.

Basic Attack

High-speed blade swipe

Heavy Attack

Fast slash attack that slashes a small distance in front of the Raven

Special Attack

Long charge up to a very large cloud swipe that deals a lot of damage

Samurai

Versatile character with a combination of distance and close-ranged attacks

Basic Attack

Fast sword swipe

Heavy Attack

Fires bow for a fast arrow projectile

Special Attack

Double Swipe, dealing more damage in a wider area

Fighter

Focuses on close-quarter fights and blocking to get close to opponents. He has the largest amount of health

Basic Attack

Punches

Heavy Attack

Blocks, receiving only half damage from all attacks when blocking

Special Attack

Kick that also shoots a fireball
